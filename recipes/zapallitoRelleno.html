Stuffed zucchini

Description

Stuffed zucchini is a hearty and flavorful dish made by hollowing out zucchini and filling them with a savory mixture of minced meat, vegetables, herbs, and cheese. Baked to perfection, these tender zucchini boats are topped with a golden, cheesy crust, making them an excellent main course or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef, chicken, or pork
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice (optional)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (optional, for baking)

Steps

  1. Wash the zucchinis and cut them in half lengthwise.
  2. Scoop out the pulp with a spoon, leaving a thin shell to form a "boat."
  3. Chop the zucchini pulp and set aside.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Sauté the onion until translucent, about 3–4 minutes.
  6. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Stir in the bell pepper and chopped zucchini pulp, cooking until softened.
  8. Add the ground meat, breaking it apart, and cook until browned and fully cooked.
  9. Season with paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  10. Optional: Stir in the cooked rice for a heartier filling
  11. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  12. Place the zucchini boats in a baking dish.
  13. Spoon the filling into each zucchini half, packing it tightly.
  14. Sprinkle the tops with Parmesan and mozzarella cheese.
  15. Optional: Pour tomato sauce into the baking dish around the zucchinis for added moisture and flavor.
  16. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes.
  17. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10–15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden.
  18. Allow the stuffed zucchinis to cool slightly before serving.
  19. Serve warm, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.
