Neapolitan-Style Milanesa with Mashed Potatoes

Description

This comforting dish combines crispy breaded beef or chicken cutlets topped with a layer of tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and a touch of oregano. Served with creamy mashed potatoes, it’s a hearty and satisfying meal that brings a classic Italian-Argentine flavor to your table.

Ingredients

  • 4 thin beef or chicken cutlets
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup sliced ham (optional)
  • 2 lbs (900 g) potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Season the beef or chicken cutlets with salt and pepper.
  2. Beat the eggs in a shallow bowl.
  3. In another shallow bowl, mix the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, oregano, and garlic powder.
  4. Dip each cutlet into the beaten eggs, then coat it evenly with the breadcrumb mixture.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the cutlets until golden brown on both sides, about 3–4 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Place the fried cutlets on a baking tray.
  8. Spread a layer of tomato sauce on each cutlet.
  9. Add a slice of ham (optional), then sprinkle mozzarella cheese and oregano on top.
  10. Bake for 10–15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Boil the potatoes in salted water until tender, about 15–20 minutes.
  12. Drain and mash the potatoes until smooth.
  13. Add butter and warm milk, mixing until creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  14. Plate the Neapolitan Milanesa alongside a generous portion of mashed potatoes.
  15. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
